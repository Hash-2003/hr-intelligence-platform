# Overtime Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy ensures that overtime work is managed efficiently, compensated fairly, and complies with all applicable employment regulations. The company encourages a healthy work-life balance and intends for overtime to be an exception rather than a standard practice.

## 2. Overtime Approval Process
* **Mandatory Prior Approval:** All overtime must be explicitly approved by the employee's direct manager *before* the overtime hours are worked. 
* **Documentation:** Employees must not assume overtime is approved under any circumstances. Valid approval must be recorded either via written communication (email) or formal authorization within the company's time-tracking system.
* **Unapproved Overtime:** Working unapproved overtime is a violation of company policy and may lead to disciplinary action.

## 3. Compliance and Compensation
* **Regulatory Alignment:** All overtime worked will be calculated and compensated in strict accordance with internal HR compensation policies and standard labor regulations.
* **Logging Hours:** Approved overtime must be logged into the timesheet system within 24 hours of the shift completion to ensure accurate payroll processing.

## 4. Escalation and Dispute Resolution
* **Workload Concerns:** If an employee finds themselves consistently required to work overtime to meet standard job expectations, they should request a workload review with their manager.
* **HR Escalation:** Repeated concerns regarding excessive overtime, pressured unapproved overtime, or compensation disputes should be escalated directly to the HR Department.
