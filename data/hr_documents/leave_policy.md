# Employee Leave Policy

## 1. Purpose and Scope
This policy outlines the guidelines and procedures regarding employee time off. The company recognizes the importance of work-life balance and provides paid leave to ensure employees can rest, recover from illness, and attend to personal matters. This policy applies to all full-time employees.

## 2. Annual Leave
* **Entitlement:** All full-time employees are entitled to 14 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Leave is accrued proportionally on a monthly basis.
* **Request Procedure:** Annual leave requests must be submitted through the HR Information System (HRIS) at least seven (7) days prior to the intended start date of the leave.
* **Approval Criteria:** Leave approval is not automatic. It is contingent upon manager confirmation and overall team availability to ensure uninterrupted business operations.

## 3. Sick Leave
* **Usage:** Sick leave is strictly for instances where an employee is unable to perform their duties due to illness, injury, or necessary medical appointments.
* **Notification:** Employees must notify their direct manager at least one hour before their standard start time on the day of absence.
* **Medical Certification:** For any sick leave extending beyond two (2) consecutive workdays, the employee must provide a valid medical certificate from a licensed healthcare professional upon returning to work. Failure to provide documentation may result in the absence being marked as unpaid.

## 4. Casual Leave
* **Usage:** Casual leave is provided to allow employees to address short, unforeseen personal matters or emergencies (e.g., family emergencies, urgent administrative tasks).
* **Limitations:** Casual leave cannot be combined with Annual Leave and should be requested as soon as the employee is aware of the need.
