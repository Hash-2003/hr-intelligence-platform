# Code of Conduct

## 1. Introduction
The company is committed to fostering a safe, inclusive, and professional work environment. This Code of Conduct outlines the ethical and professional standards expected of all employees, contractors, and partners representing the organization.

## 2. Professionalism and Respect
* Employees are expected to act with integrity, professionalism, and respect in all interactions with colleagues, clients, and external stakeholders.
* Collaborative and constructive communication is the standard; hostile, aggressive, or demeaning behavior is strictly prohibited.

## 3. Anti-Harassment and Non-Discrimination
* **Zero Tolerance:** The company maintains a zero-tolerance policy regarding harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and retaliation. 
* **Protected Characteristics:** This applies to behavior targeting any individual based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic.
* **Retaliation:** Employees who report misconduct in good faith are protected from any form of retaliation.

## 4. Confidentiality and Data Security
* **Information Protection:** Employees must vigorously protect confidential company assets, proprietary business strategies, and sensitive employee data.
* **Data Sharing:** Confidential information must not be shared with unauthorized internal or external parties. This obligation persists even after employment terminates.

## 5. Reporting Mechanism
* **Filing a Complaint:** Employees who witness or experience violations of this Code of Conduct should report the incident through the appropriate HR channel immediately.
* **Investigation:** All complaints will be handled discreetly and investigated promptly by the HR Department.
